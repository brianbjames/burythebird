About

Katie M. Flynn is a writer, editor, and educator based in San Francisco. Her short fiction has appeared in Colorado Review, Indiana Review, Masters Review, Ninth Letter, Tin House, Witness Magazine, and elsewhere. She has been nominated for a Pushcart twice and is the recipient of Colorado Review’s Nelligan Prize for Short Fiction and a Steinbeck fellowship in Creative Writing. Currently, she is a fellow at the San Francisco Writers’ Grotto and serves as fiction editor at Split Lip Magazine. She recently finished her first novel about love, revenge, and uploaded consciousness, the first chapter of which appears in Indiana Review.
