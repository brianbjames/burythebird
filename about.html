About

Katie M. Flynn is a writer, editor, and educator based in San Francisco. Her short fiction has appeared in Colorado Review, Indiana Review, The Masters Review, Ninth Letter, Tin House, Witness Magazine, and elsewhere. She’s been awarded Colorado Review’s Nelligan Prize for Short Fiction, a fellowship from the San Francisco Writers' Grotto, and the Steinbeck Fellowship in Creative Writing. She recently finished her first novel about love, revenge, and uploaded consciousness. Katie also serves as fiction editor at Split Lip Magazine.
