Editing

For Creative Writers: Katie offers comprehensive manuscript evaluations for essays, short stories, and book-length projects. She also works with writers on manuscripts-in-progress, offering developmental feedback and ongoing support.

Katie holds an MFA in Writing from the University of San Francisco and has taught college-level creative writing for over ten years, as well as online writing workshops through literary journals. She was fiction editor at The Indianola Review and currently serves as fiction editor at Split Lip Magazine, as well as a nonfiction reader at New England Review.

Please note: Katie cannot consider any pieces she has edited for publication at the literary journals where she works.

For Academic Writers: Katie works with graduate students and professionals in an array of fields including: Humanities, Social Sciences, Psychology, Education, Business, and Management. She supports writers with academic papers and book-length projects, graduate theses and dissertations, and personal statements for graduate school applications.

With graduate degrees in Writing and Geography, Katie is comfortable with a broad range of material and styles, including MLA, APA, and Chicago. For over a decade, Katie worked as a college writing instructor and writing center director, supporting hundreds of student writers one-on-one, including second language learners. She has also worked with academics as a freelance copyeditor on journal articles, book reviews and proposals, and book-length projects.
