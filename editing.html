Editing

Katie offers editing in the areas of academic and creative writing. With an MA in geography and an MFA in writing, Katie works with students and professionals in an array of fields including: Humanities, Social Sciences, Psychology, Education, Business, and Management. She supports writers with academic papers and book-length projects, graduate theses and dissertations, and personal statements for graduate school applications.

Katie also works with fiction writers both in the capacity of editor and coach. For writers with completed manuscripts, she offers comprehensive manuscript evaluations, as well as detailed line editing and proofreading services. She also works with writers on manuscripts in progress, offering developmental feedback and ongoing support.
